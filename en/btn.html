Exterior

The project consists of 9 2-story houses and 6 mini-apartments, located in a diversified utility area

Interior

Each house has an area of 81m2 with 2 floors, consists of 6 rooms with mezzanines, and a spacious backyard for parking. Each room includes a kitchen and a bathroom which is convenient for rent.
