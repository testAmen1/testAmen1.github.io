FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS BDS TOKEN?

BDS is a digital utility token created on KardiaChain (KRC20) and Binance Smart Chain (BEP20). Briefly, BDS is a REWARD for application in real estate investment and business.

WHICH ORGANIZATION ISSUES AND DEPLOYS BDS TOKEN?

BDS token is issued by BDS Holding LLC – a technology and investment enterprise located in the state of Georgia, USA with the goal of creating a technology platform to support real estate business and investment around the world. In Vietnam, BDS Holding LLC chooses to cooperate with Big Group as a Deployment Partner to apply BDS Token into the real estate business and investment ecosystem. Big Group is known as an enterprise with a history of more than 10 years of establishment, a pioneer in the model of building a real estate community with a management board and a team of advisors consisting of reputable experts with a high level of expertise in this field

HOW TO OWN BDS TOKEN?

BDS is a Utility Token serving as reward point. To own BDS, you can use the method of exchange from other digital tokens like USDT to BDS on electronic exchanges that accept the conversion between these digital tokens. Exchanging digital assets as BDS/USDT has become simple and popular on many CEX and DEX.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF HOLDING BDS TOKENS?

BDS Token is the native token of a comprehensive real estate ecosystem on BDS INVEST technology platform. Holding BDS, you can participate in subdivided real estate projects or participate in real estate brokerage activities, investment training programs, etc. available on these platforms. The first platform to apply BDS is BDS INVEST. When you use BDS token to participate in real estate investment and business, the REAL ESTATE PROJECTS will provide you with the actual INVESTMENT PROFIT and BUSINESS COMMISSION. BDS is a tool for making your participation simple and easy with advanced technology.

WHAT MAKES BDS INVEST PLATFORM DIFFERENT FROM OTHER REAL ESTATE APPLICATIONS?

As the pioneer platform to apply the preeminent 4.0 Blockchain technology through BDS digital token, BDS INVEST brings a comprehensive solution for Real Estate Investors, Brokers, and Project Owners when using this technology. With BDS INVEST, you can:
- 4.0 Investment: Utilizing arbitrary small capital to participate in large real estate projects, enjoying profits from investment, easily liquidating the investment by smart electronic exchange forms.
- 4.0 Accumulation: Accumulating small investments to gradually reach the full ownership scale, and then converting electronic tokens into assets such as land, houses, and apartments with clear and transparent legal information.
- 4.0 Broker: Introducing to friends to participate in effective projects by a smart affiliate marketing link, ensuring instant commissions with clear and transparent information.
- 4.0 Conversion: Easily converting digital assets that are electronic tokens to actual real estate products such as houses and land with support and advice from leading experts
In addition, if you are Project Owner, you can also contact our support team to participate in the digitization of your real estate projects on the BDS Invest platform.

HOW CAN I START PARTICIPATION IN BDS INVEST PLATFORM?

- For general users: For the simplest participation, you can immediately download the BDS INVEST application available on Google and Apple Store to open an account and follow the instructions on the application to participate.
- For users knowledgeable about Blockchain: You can access Dapps Websites and connect your Blockchain Wallets to be able to Own and Exchange easily between BDS Tokens with other popular digital assets and start your own journey to discover 4.0 real estate technology.
